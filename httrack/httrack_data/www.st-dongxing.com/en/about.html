Profile

    Dongxing Hotel Supplies Co., Ltd. of Shantou is a comprehensive manufacturer of hotel amenities which integrates the activities of printing, plastic injection, plastic bottles, liquid, soap, toothbrush and linen. We have been focusing on research and development of guest amenities for high-end hotels for 30 years. As an experienced supplier for more than 500 domestic and international luxury hotels (groups) and hotel management companies, we have established supply network throughout Asia Pacific, Europe and America.

    The manufacturing facility covers 30,000 square meters including a GMPC certified cosmetic workshop with 2,000 square meters. Advanced international toothbrush manufacturing machines "ZAHORANSKY" are applied to good quality toothbrush. The whole production process in our factory is supervised in compliance with international environment and quality standards.

Since the foundation of our company, we have been committed to the fulfillment of "high quality, environmental protection, brand building, good service and innovation". While developing business, the company doesn’t ignore the corporate social responsibility and a certain percentage of yearly revenue is allocated to development and research on environmentally friendly products. We are the first manufacturer in our industry to introduce and apply PLA (polylactic acid) to replace normal plastic material. The goal of our company is to promote high-end branded products to hotels and bring green and luxury lifestyle to the society.  Meanwhile, we have developed home brands like Clear Dew , Yichuner and Shalu.
